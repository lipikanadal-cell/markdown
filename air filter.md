# How to Replace Air Filter in Toyota Prius 2020
Replace the engine air filter in a Toyota Prius to maintain engine performance and fuel efficiency. 

## Estimated Time
10–15 minutes

## Tools Required
No tools required (in most cases)

## Prerequisites
1. Ensure the vehicle is parked on a flat surface. 
2. Check whether the engine is turned off and cool.

## Context
The engine air filter is located inside a rectangular plastic housing in the engine compartment.

## Task
Step 1: Open the hood of the vehicle.

Step 2: Locate the engine air filter housing.

![Air filter](engine_ariflter.png)

Step 3: Unfasten the clips or latches on the air filter housing.
**Do not remove the housing completely**.

Step 4: Remove the old air filter from the housing.
**Note the orientation of the filter for correct installation.**


Step 5: Insert the new air filter into the housing.
**Ensure the filter sits evenly and the seal fits securely around the edges.**

Step 6: Close the air filter housing and secure all clips.

Step 7: Close the hood of the vehicle

## Verification

- Start the engine and listen for unusual sounds. 

- Ensure the hood is properly secured.

## Outcome
The engine air filter has been successfully replaced.
